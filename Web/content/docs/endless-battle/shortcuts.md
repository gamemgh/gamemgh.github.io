# %title%
There are a large number of shortcuts used in the game, and are thus divided into categories.

## Movement and Navigation
- `Q`: Turn left 45 degrees.
	- On mobile, swipe left with 3 fingers.
	- Combine with `Shift` key will turn by 90 degrees, mobile touch not available.
- `E`: Turn right 45 degrees.
	- On mobile, swipe right with 3 fingers.
	- Combine with `Shift` key will turn by 90 degrees, mobile touch not available.
- `S`: Turn by a full 180 degrees, mobile touch not available.
- `F`: Checks direction.
- `Spacebar`: Jumps.
	- On mobile, swipe down with 2 fingers.
- Arrow keys: Move around the map. Holding down one of them will move continuously to that direction.
	- On mobile, swipe to the direction you want to move. You may also hold down to move continuously after the swipe.

## General / Miscellaneous
- `R`: Reloads the selected weapon.
	- On mobile, triple tap with 3 fingers.
- `D`: Brings up a menu to select a weapon to draw.
	- On mobile, triple tap with 2 fingers.
- `H`: Checks health.
	- On mobile, swipe up with 2 fingers.
- `L`: Checks resurrection, level, and experience.
	- On mobile, double tap with 3 fingers.
- `Z`: Checks ammo, if available.
	- On mobile, swipe up with 3 fingers.
- `M`: Brings up a menu where you can cast spells if available.
	- On mobile, double tap with one finger on the top right of the screen.
- Either Control: Fires the selected weapon.
	- On mobile, single tap with one finger.
- `Escape`: Exit to main menu.
	- On mobile, click back button.

## Inventory
- `Tab`: Next item.
	- On mobile, swipe right with 2 fingers.
- `Shift+Tab`: Previous item.
	- On mobile, swipe left with 2 fingers.
- `Enter`: Uses the selected item.
	- On mobile, double tap with 2 fingers.
